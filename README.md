# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-Tableau

📊 HR Analytics Dashboard using Tableau




📌 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce trends, and key HR metrics. The dashboard helps HR professionals and business managers make data-driven decisions by visualizing employee data through KPI cards, interactive filters, and insightful charts.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles.
Monitor workforce demographics and employee satisfaction.
Identify trends affecting employee retention.
Provide interactive business insights for HR decision-making.



📉 Dashboard Insights
Research & Development recorded the highest employee attrition.
Sales Executive and Laboratory Technician roles experienced the highest attrition.
Single employees showed the highest attrition among marital status groups.
Male employees accounted for a larger share of employee attrition than female employees.
Most employees did not work overtime, while a smaller percentage regularly worked overtime.
